About Tomato Rice

Tomato rice is a simply,flavorful & delicious one pot Indian dish made with rice, ripe tomatoes, spices &herbs. This tomato rice can be made in a stovetop pressure cooker, instant pot, electric rice cooker or even in regular pot, if you are looking to use up left over or precooked white rice, you can make tomato rice just under 20 mins following this.This Tomato rice can be served with out any sides as it is very fiavorful & tasty on its own.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED

  1. 1 cup basmati rice
  2. 2 Tablespoons oil
  3. onions
  4. 2 green chilles
  5. 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  6. 1 cup tomatoes
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 1 teaspoon chili powder

INSTRUCTIONS

Wash 1 cup rice very well a few times until the water runs clear. Soak the rice if you wish until the preparation is done. Later drain the rice completely and set aside.

Wash and slice 1 medium onion thinly or chop them. Also slit 1 green chili.Rins & chop or puree 2 to 3 medium tomatoes. I use about 1 cup chopped tomatoes (200 grams).

Grate or crush half inch gingre & 2 garlic cloves to make 1 tablespoon paste. Set aside.
